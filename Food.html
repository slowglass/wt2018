Food

  • Dim Sun contains wheat and may contain milk.
  • Melba toast and french and brown bread all contain wheat.
  • The rye thins are 100 percent rye.
  • Tortilla chiips are Maize flour though not claiming gluten free so have potential factory contamination.
  • Kettle chips are gluten free.
  • Chorizo is wheat and lactose free.
  • Sausion Sec is wheat free but contains lactose.
  • Cumberland sausages contain wheat based rusk.
  • The bread for salmon is spread with salt free butter.
  • Mushroom salad contains Parmesan.
  • Chicory and blue cheese salad obviously contains a blue cheese.
  • Roast cauliflower may have been roasted in butter other veg and omelette are all done in vegetable oils (olive / rape seed).
  • The spices on the cauliflower are just that e.g. sumac, cumin, coriander etc no wheat I am aware of.
  • Hummus/Tahini sauce -are wheat and lactose free but contain garlic.
  • Guacamole has only avocados, onion , garlic , chilli.
  • Chicken were bought ready done and I have no idea what there might be on the skins.
  • Beef is home cooked and is just beef.
